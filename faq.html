Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?

Click the invite link at the top of the page or ask a staff member for an invite!

Who is Kaboodle?

Kaboodle is the content creator and owner of the server. She streams, creates content, and brings the community together.

How can I become a mod?

Keep being active, helpful, and positive! When applications open, you can apply or be nominated by staff.

Where can I find the rules?

Check the #rules channel on Discord or ask a staff member for more info.

Who do I contact for help?

Any staff member is happy to help! You can also use the #help channel on Discord.
