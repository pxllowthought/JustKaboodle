Frequently Asked Questions

▼ How do I join the server?

Click the Kaboodle's Burrow in the upper left and you will be redircted to a link to join the server!

▼ Who is Kaboodle?

Kaboodle is the content creator and owner of the server. She streams, creates content, and brings the community together.

▼ How can I become a mod?

To become a moderator you will have to apply when the applications are open, that happens whenever new moderators are needed. You will need to be 16+ in age to be able to apply however

▼ Where can I find the rules?

Check the #rules channel on Discord, that is where you can read all our rules!

▼ Who do I contact for help?

Any staff member is happy to help! You can also create a staff ticket in the discord in #opem-discord-tickets.
