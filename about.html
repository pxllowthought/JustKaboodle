About Kaboodle's Burrow

Kaboodle's Burrow is a discord server that revolves around the streamer and youtuber named Kaboodle who does variety of content! you can find her socials under Kab's Linktree!

We host regular events, game nights, and streams, and encourage everyone to participate and make new friends. Our staff team is dedicated to keeping the community welcoming and safe for all.

Whether you're here for announcements regarding new videos, to get the newest Kaboodle news, or just to chat, we're glad to have you! If you have any questions or need help, feel free to reach out to our staff!

Welcome to the Burrow and have a warm and welcoming stay!
