About Kaboodle's Burrow

▼ What is Kabboodle's Burrow?

Kaboodle's Burrow is a vibrant, inclusive Discord community built around the content creator Kaboodle. Our server is a safe space for fans to connect, share, and have fun together.

▼ About Kaboodle

Kaboodle is a content creator who streams, creates videos, and brings people together through her positive energy and creativity. You can find her links here

▼ The Community

The Kaboodle Community has many various members, but mainly just people who enjoy the content Kaboodle makes. Our server is a safe space for all the people that would like to have some time away from their IRL. Our server accepts everyone regardless of their skin color, sexuality or disabilities. We are a large server that offers systems the option to be safe!

▼ Can I just simply join and be part of the community?

Yes! Of course, all you gotta do is join #general and start talking with us! If you feel like people are singling you out be sure to immediately reprt it to the staff!
