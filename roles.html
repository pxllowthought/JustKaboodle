Our Server Roles

Our Discord server has a variety of roles to help organize the community and recognize contributions. Here are some of the main roles you might see:

▼ Staff Roles
  • Kaboodle: The owner and heart of the community.
  • Admins: Manage the server and make important decisions.
  • Head Mods: Oversee the moderation team and help with staff management.
  • Mods: Keep the server safe and help members.
  • Trial Mods: New moderators in training.
  • Head Stream Mods: Helps manage the Twitch Mods on the team and most Twitch issues..
  • Stream Mods: These people help moderate Kab's Twitch chat. Do not ping them for any server-related issues!
▼ Special Roles
  • Creator: This role is personally given to any creator Kab follows or is a friend of hers. There is no application process for this. Do not ask for this.
  • Content Team: These are the people who help maintain Kab's YouTube channel. Do not ping them for any server-related issues.
  • Channel Artists: These are Kab's official channel artists! They make all of the art for her VODs channel, merch and more.
