Server Roles

Our Discord server has a variety of roles to help organize the community and recognize contributions. Here are some of the main roles you might see:

  • Kaboodle: Kaboodle is the owner and the heart of the community..
  • Administration Staff: In charge of behind-the-scenes server management. This includes discord bots, channel management, and more!
  • Head Mods: Helps manage the Discord Mods alongside the Admins!
  • Mods: In charge of watching the chat and keeping the server in check. They also help answer tickets. Make sure to respect them!
  • Trial Mods: New moderators in training who are new to the staff team, please be sure to remain kind to them!.
  • Stream Staff: Members who moderate the Kaboodle twitch channel.
  • The Gang: All the wonderful members who make the Burrow special!
