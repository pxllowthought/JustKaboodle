Kaboodle's Burrow Server Rules

▼ 1. Follow the Discord TOS at all times.

Please be sure to always follow the Discord Terms of Service

▼ 2. Be respectful to everyone

No hate speech of any kind is tolerated. This includes homophobia, transphobia, racism, sexism or any kind of harassment.

▼ 3. Do not ping Kaboodle or Creators.

Pinging and DMing Kaboodle without her permission is not allowed and will result in punishment. This also includes excessively pinging staff or other content creators.

▼ 4. Do not direct message staff members without permission.

Show respect to other members of the server, the staff in this server, and Kaboodle's Twitch chat. Along with this, do not DM Twitch or Discord Staff; the staff are not messengers. If you have any issues or questions in the server, open a ticket. If you DM any staff member without said staff member's permission first, you will receive a warning. (Look at Rule 3)

▼ 5. No politics.

Please keep all talks about politics to a minimum.

▼ 6. No NSFW or inappropriate content.

Any form of talk about drugs will be deleted if said. As well as NSFW content and slurs will result in a ban. This is a safe and welcoming server.

▼ 7. No self-promotion or advertising.

This is not a self-promotion server. Any form of self-promotion will result in punishment and unsolicited promotion through a member's DM's is a bannable offence.

▼ 8. Other langages are besides English are not allowed.

This is an English-speaking server only. To ensure mods do not have to translate, please keep all conversations to English only.

▼ 9. Spam

Keep spam to a minimum. Excessive spam will result in punishment.

▼ 10. Spoilers

Place warnings (spoilers) on all seizure-inducing material, possible triggering content, and anything you're unsure of. This can be done by putting || on either side of the emote or placing a spoiler on an image.

▼ 11. Keep the minimodding to a minimum.

The mods know what they are doing! If we miss anything, feel free to open a ticket and bring it to our attention but do not act on our behalf.

▼ 12. Loopholes

Don't try to use loopholes to get out of these rules. The staff has the final say on punishments especially if they are to violate the rules or get around them.
